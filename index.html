Whispers of the Moon: A Witch's Tale

Blurb: Millicent Hawthorne's peaceful life shatters when her husband succumbs to a plague.
Unable to grieve, she soon has to seek revenge for her daughter's death at the hands of the sinister Fredrick Griffin. Turning to forbidden witchcraft, Millicent makes a pact with Morgana, a powerful birth witch, unintentionally triggering a curse woven with wolf's blood and ancient magic.
This curse reshapes the fates of unknowing descendants. "Whispers of the Moon: A Witch's Tale" unfolds—a story of revenge, unintended consequences, and the enduring force of dark magic.
Prequel to The Alpha's Babies, but both can be read as stand-alone.

Debt to the Mafia

Blurb: What if the last drop of family you had left, your mother, chose to pay her debts with you?
Meet Elena De'Leon; her mother has made her the currency for a deal with the notorious mafia kingpin, Leon Martinez. Leon Martinez, wearied by the incessant carousel of fleeting affairs as a mafia boss, has reached a turning point. Now, he craves not just any woman, but the daughter of the very woman indebted to him. In this precarious exchange, desire intertwines with debt, setting the stage for a perilous dance of hearts and debts.

The Mistress' Revenge

Blurb: In the glittering world of a high-tech empire, Samantha's love story with CEO Peterson Charles hides a dark secret. As their dreams of a future together unfold, so does Peterson's hidden life—a double existence that threatens to shatter Samantha's world. Unbeknownst to her, a mysterious rival lurks in the shadows, unraveling a web of deceit and betrayal. The revelation sparks a relentless pursuit of the truth, setting the stage for a gripping tale of love, revenge, and the shocking consequences of a tech magnate's hidden life. Brace yourself for a journey where passion collides with deception, and trust becomes the ultimate casualty.
